About Lukes

Luke’s Diner is a cozy eatery nestled on the town square in Stars Hollow, Connecticut. Formerly William’s Hardware (owned by Luke’s father), Luke and his father turned the old hardware into a diner, that now serves burgers, fries, sandwiches, pies, coffee, and on special occasions chocolate chip pancakes.
